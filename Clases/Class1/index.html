Potato Tortilla Recipe:
Ingredients: 4 medium potatoes (thinly sliced), 1 onion (thinly sliced), 6 eggs, salt, pepper, olive oil. Prep Potatoes: Heat olive oil in a non-stick skillet, add potatoes and onions. Cook on medium-low until tender (about 15 min). Whisk Eggs: In a bowl, whisk eggs and season with salt and pepper. Combine: Add the cooked potatoes and onions to the egg mixture. Let them soak for a few minutes. Heat Pan: Wipe the skillet and add a bit of olive oil. Heat on medium. Cook Mixture: Pour the egg-potato mixture into the skillet. Cook for 5-7 min, gently lifting edges to let uncooked egg flow underneath. Flip Tortilla: Place a plate over the skillet, carefully flip the tortilla onto the plate. Slide Back: Slide the tortilla back into the skillet and cook for another 5-7 min. Serve: Slide the cooked tortilla onto a plate. Let it cool slightly before slicing. Enjoy: Serve warm or at room temperature as a delicious potato tortilla.

Enjoy your homemade potato tortilla!
